#Platformania!

## Overview

Welcome to Platformania! , an exciting and challenging platformer game that will test your skills and reflexes! In this game, you'll navigate through various levels, overcoming obstacles and collecting rewards to achieve victory. Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure that will keep you entertained for hours.

## Game Features

- Dynamic Platforming: Leap across moving platforms, strategically time your jumps, and navigate through challenging levels.
- Unique Obstacles: Face a variety of obstacles, from wide canyons to ground obstacles, adding depth and excitement to the gameplay.
- Reward System: Collect rewards and power-ups to enhance your gaming experience and progress through the levels.

## How to Play

1. Use the arrow keys to control your character's movement.
2. Jump over obstacles and onto platforms to progress through the levels.
3. Collect rewards and power-ups to boost your performance and increase your chances of success.
4. Press spacebar to shoot at enemies

## Installation

1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Launch Platformania! using a live-server plugin in your IDE/text-editor
3. Enjoy the exciting gameplay and challenge yourself to reach the highest score!
